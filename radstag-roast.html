Radstag Roast

Slow-roasted game meat with herbs, perfect for campfires and feasts.

← Back to all recipes

Ingredients

  • 1 kg venison or beef roast
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tsp dried rosemary
  • 1 tsp dried thyme
  • Salt and black pepper
  • 1 cup broth or water

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 170°C (340°F).
  2. Rub the roast with oil, garlic, rosemary, thyme, salt, and pepper.
  3. Place the meat in a roasting pan and add broth to the bottom.
  4. Cover and roast for 1.5–2 hours, basting occasionally, until tender.
  5. Let rest for 10 minutes, then slice thinly and serve with the pan juices.

Wasteland Tip

If cooking over a campfire, wrap seasoned meat in foil and cook slowly over hot coals.
