Nuka-Cola Float

A fizzy, sweet dessert drink inspired by the most famous soda in the wasteland.

← Back to all recipes

Ingredients

  • 1 glass of cola
  • 2 scoops vanilla ice cream
  • Whipped cream (optional)
  • Cherry on top (optional)

Instructions

  1. Fill a tall glass about two-thirds full with cola.
  2. Gently add the ice cream scoops to the glass.
  3. Top with whipped cream and a cherry if available.
  4. Serve immediately with a straw and spoon.

Wasteland Tip

Try different soda flavors to create rare “variant” Nuka-Colas, just like in the game.
