Deathclaw Steak

A powerful, protein-packed steak worthy of only the bravest vault dwellers.

← Back to all recipes

Ingredients

  • 2 large steaks (any thick-cut beef)
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, smashed
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • Salt and plenty of black pepper
  • Fresh herbs (thyme or rosemary)

Instructions

  1. Season steaks generously with salt and pepper.
  2. Heat oil in a heavy skillet over high heat.
  3. Sear steaks for 2–3 minutes per side.
  4. Reduce heat to medium, add butter, garlic, and herbs.
  5. Baste steaks with the melted butter for another 2–3 minutes, until desired doneness.
  6. Rest steaks for 5 minutes before serving.

Wasteland Tip

Pair with roasted potatoes or mutfruit salad to recover from your Deathclaw encounter.
