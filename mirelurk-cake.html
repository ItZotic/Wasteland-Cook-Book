Mirelurk Cake

Crispy patties made with flaky “Mirelurk” meat, inspired by coastal wasteland cuisine.

← Back to all recipes

Ingredients

  • 400 g cooked crab or fish meat, flaked
  • 1 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1 egg
  • 2 tbsp mayonnaise
  • 1 tbsp mustard
  • 2 green onions, finely chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Oil for pan-frying

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, combine the crab or fish meat, breadcrumbs, egg, mayonnaise, mustard, and green onions.
  2. Season with salt and pepper, then mix until the mixture holds together when pressed.
  3. Shape into small patties, about the size of your palm.
  4. Heat oil in a skillet over medium heat.
  5. Cook patties for 3–4 minutes per side, until golden brown and heated through.
  6. Serve hot with lemon wedges or your favorite dipping sauce.

Wasteland Tip

Any flaky canned fish makes a good stand-in if fresh Mirelurks are in short supply.
