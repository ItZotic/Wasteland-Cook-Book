Iguana on a Stick

Simple skewered meat, grilled over open flames and perfect for street vendors.

← Back to all recipes

Ingredients

  • 500 g boneless meat (chicken, pork, or beef), cut into cubes
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp honey or sugar
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • Skewers

Instructions

  1. Soak wooden skewers in water for 20 minutes if using.
  2. In a bowl, mix oil, soy sauce, honey, and garlic.
  3. Add meat and stir to coat. Marinate for at least 30 minutes.
  4. Thread meat onto skewers.
  5. Grill over medium heat, turning occasionally, for 8–10 minutes or until cooked through.
  6. Serve hot, straight off the stick.

Wasteland Tip

Skewer any foraged vegetables alongside the meat to stretch your rations.
